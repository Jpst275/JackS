How to Make the Dough

Step 1 - Gather the ingriedients
1000 grams of Flour
10 grams Yeast
700 grams of Water
30 grams of Salt
Olive Oil

Step 2 - Place the yeast, 1 teaspoon of the flour, sugar and warm water into a small bowl or jug and whisk together well. Allow to stand in a warm place until the yeast activates, this will take roughly 5â€“10 minutes.

Step 3 - Place the remaining flour and salt into a large mixing bowl, make a well in the centre, then pour in the yeast mixture and slowly combine, gradually bringing more and more of the dry mixture into the centre until a stiff dough forms.

Step 4 - Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and drizzle the dough with olive oil. Knead the olive oil into the dough until the dough is shiny and bounces back to touch, this will take approximately 5 minutes.

Step 5 - Lightly grease a large bowl, place the dough in and cover with cling film. Leave bowl in a warm place to rise for half an hour before rolling out as a pizza base.
