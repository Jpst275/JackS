Make the Perfect Pizza

Step 1 - Gather your ingredients
2 Cups of Flour
2 Cups of Yeast
1 1/2 Cups of Water
1/2 Cup of Pizza Sauce
2 Cups of Mozarella Cheese
Toppings of your choice

Step 2 - Preheat the oven

Step 3 - Get a clean board and place it on a flat surface

Step 4 - Get dough and roll it out on a board evenly into your desired shape

Step 5 - Get tomato sauce and spread it out on the dough evenly using a big spoon

Step 6 - Sprinkle 2/3 of the cheese on the tomato sauce

Step 7 - Add your chosen toppings and spread them evenly around the sauce

Step 8 - Add the rest of the cheese

Step 9 - Place pizza into oven and cook until cheese has melted and become golden brown

Step 10 - Remove the pizza from the oven, slice it into 8 pieces and enjoy
