Best seen in Half Screen

Step 1 - Mine a tree with your fists and collect the wood

Step 2 - Open your inventory with 'E' for computer or 'Y' for X Box and craft wooden planks

Step 3 - Using the wooden planks make a crafting table and 12 sticks

Step 4 - Place down the crafting table and using the sticks and the remaining wooden planks craft a wooden pickaxe

Step 5 - Mine down until you hit stone and collect 11 blocks of cobblestone

Step 6 - Using your crafting table, cobblestone and the remaining sticks craft a stone pickaxe, stone axe, stone sword, and a stone shovel

Step 7 - Find a cave and find 11 blocks of iron ore and mine it using your stone pickaxe

Step 8 - Mine 9 blocks of cobblestone and craft a furnace

Step 9 - Place the remaining wood planks and the iron ore into the furnace

Step 10 - Collect the iron and craft more sticks and craft a iron pickaxe, iron axe, iron sword, and an iron shovel
